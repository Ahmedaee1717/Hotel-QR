Staff Instructions:

Please read the consent agreement to the guest and have them sign on the touchscreen. The guest must understand they can opt-out at any time and their data will be deleted.

Biometric Data Consent for OnePass Face Recognition

Purpose: I consent to the collection and use of my facial biometric data for the purpose of verifying my identity and providing access to hotel facilities and services during my stay.

Data Collected: A mathematical template will be created from my facial photograph. This template cannot be used to recreate my photo and is used only for identity verification.

Data Storage: My biometric data will be stored securely on encrypted servers and will not be shared with third parties.

Data Retention: My biometric data will be automatically deleted 24 hours after my checkout date or immediately upon request.

Right to Withdraw: I understand that I can withdraw my consent at any time through the guest portal or by requesting staff assistance. Upon withdrawal, my biometric data will be permanently deleted within 24 hours.

Alternative Access: I understand that I can choose to use QR code verification instead of facial recognition at any time without any disadvantage to my service.

GDPR/BIPA Compliance: This collection complies with GDPR, BIPA, and applicable biometric privacy laws.
