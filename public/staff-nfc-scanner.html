How to Use NFC Scanner

  • Ensure your device has NFC enabled in settings
  • Click "Start NFC Scan" button above
  • Hold guest's NFC card close to your device (usually back of phone)
  • Wait for verification confirmation

Note: Web NFC API is supported on Android devices with Chrome/Edge browsers. iOS devices require native NFC app.
